The method

Three words. One long game.

Not a strategy you'll abandon by Friday — a way of running an account that still stands a year from now.

01

Protect

Capital comes first. Risk is defined before the trade, not during it — so no single loss can wreck a week, and no revenge trade can wreck a year.

02

Master

Trade the plan, not the mood. Score yourself on execution, not on the result of any one trade. A good trade can lose; a reckless one can win on luck.

03

Grow

Slow, compounding, repeatable. A business, not a lottery ticket. Size grows only as the account earns it — steadiness compounds, volatility just leaks.
